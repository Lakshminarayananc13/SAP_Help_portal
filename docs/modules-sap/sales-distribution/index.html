SAP SD (Sales and Distribution)

SAP Sales and Distribution (SD) is a core SAP module that manages the complete sales process, from receiving customer inquiries to delivering products and generating invoices. It helps organizations efficiently manage sales, shipping, billing, and customer-related activities.

Overview of SAP SD​

SAP SD supports the order-to-cash (O2C) process by managing customer orders, deliveries, billing, and payments.

FeatureDescription
Sales Order ProcessingManaging customer orders
Shipping & DeliveryHandling product delivery
BillingCreating customer invoices
Customer ManagementManaging customer master data
PricingDetermining product prices and discounts

Key Components of SAP SD​

Sales​

The Sales component manages customer inquiries, quotations, and sales orders.

ActivityDescription
InquiryCustomer request for information
QuotationPrice offer provided to a customer
Sales OrderCustomer order for products or services
ContractLong-term agreement with a customer
Scheduling AgreementDelivery schedule agreement with a customer

Shipping​

Shipping manages the delivery of products to customers.

ActivityDescription
Delivery CreationGenerate delivery document
PickingSelect products from inventory
PackingPack products for shipment
Goods IssueRecord shipment of goods
TransportationManage shipment and logistics

Billing​

Billing creates invoices for delivered goods and services.

Billing TypeDescription
InvoiceStandard customer billing document
Credit MemoRefund or reduction in customer charges
Debit MemoAdditional charge to customer
Cancellation InvoiceReversal of an invoice

Customer Master​

Customer Master stores all information related to customers.

Data TypeDescription
General DataCustomer name, address, contact details
Company Code DataFinancial and accounting information
Sales Area DataSales-specific information

Pricing​

Pricing determines the final amount charged to customers.

Pricing ElementDescription
Base PriceProduct selling price
DiscountReduction in price
SurchargeAdditional charge
TaxApplicable taxes
FreightTransportation charges

Sales Order Process​

StepProcess
1Customer Inquiry
2Quotation Creation
3Sales Order Creation
4Delivery Processing
5Goods Issue
6Billing

Important SAP SD Documents​

DocumentPurpose
InquiryCustomer request for information
QuotationPrice proposal to customer
Sales OrderCustomer purchase order
Delivery DocumentShipment details
Billing DocumentCustomer invoice
Credit MemoCustomer refund document

Order-to-Cash (O2C)​

Order-to-Cash (O2C) is the end-to-end process that starts when a customer places an order and ends when payment is received.

O2C Flow​

  1. Customer Inquiry
  2. Quotation
  3. Sales Order
  4. Delivery
  5. Goods Issue
  6. Billing
  7. Payment Receipt

The O2C process ensures smooth sales operations, accurate billing, and timely revenue recognition.
